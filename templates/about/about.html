About Re.nooble ...

We, Alvaro and Hannes, truely believe in renewable energies, especially in small scale renewable technologies.
We think that nations move too slow when it comes to realizing the climate targets on a national level, and we see many people moving on and installing their own systems in their backyards, on their roof-tops, on balcoonies, etc. We want to create a platform for the early adopters to spread their experiences and motivate others to join the energy movement and produce clean, sustainable, reliable and most important affortable renewable power.
