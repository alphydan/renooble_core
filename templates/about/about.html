About Re.nooble ...

We, Alvaro and Hannes, truely believe in renewable energies, especially in small scale renewable technologies.
We think that nations move too slow when it comes to realizing the climate targets on a national level, and we see many people moving on and installing their own systems in their backyards, on their roof-tops, on balcoonies, etc. We want to create a platform for the early adopters to spread their experiences and motivate others to join the energy movement and produce clean, sustainable, reliable and most important affortable renewable power.

Alvaro ... is a French-Spanish quantum physist, who devoted his time to promote renewable energies. He is passionate about energy questions and especially peak oil. Alvaro is a founder of re.nooble and a full-time re.nooblist. He devotes his main time to push forward the idea of re.nooble and realize the idea of re.nooble.
In the project, Alvaro is the chief strategist, chief developer, chief designer and ... all together; he is the pillar behind re.nooble.

Hannes ... is a German electrical engineer, who believes that we should be generating energy more consious, local and at the real cost. Hannes is a founder of re.nooble and a part-time re.nooblist. During the day time, he works for "big business" in the energy sector. During the night time, he turns into a full time re.nooblist.
He devoted his latest times to the software philosophies of Django and Pinax, so that re.nooble can take most use of the online tools.
